About Me

Jejen J Abidin

Fullstack Developer & Enthusiasm RPA

Hi, my name is Jejen J Abidin and I began using WordPress when it first began. I am a skilled and versatile Full Stack Web Developer with expertise in developing robust and efficient web applications. Additionally, I possess proficiency in developing automation solutions using the UIPath RPA platform. With a passion for creating innovative and user-friendly interfaces, I have successfully delivered projects across various industries.

Web Development:

In my role as a Full Stack Web Developer, I have demonstrated proficiency in both frontend and backend development. I have extensive experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern JavaScript frameworks like React and Angular. My strong understanding of responsive design principles ensures that the websites and applications I develop are optimized for different devices and screen sizes. I have also worked with backend technologies such as Node.js, Express, and databases like MongoDB and MySQL.

Throughout my career, I have collaborated with cross-functional teams to gather requirements, design architecture, and implement scalable and secure web solutions. I am adept at using version control systems like Git and employing agile methodologies to ensure efficient project management and timely delivery. My problem-solving skills and attention to detail enable me to identify and resolve issues efficiently, ensuring smooth operation and an enhanced user experience.

RPA (UIPath):

In addition to web development, I have specialized knowledge in Robotic Process Automation (RPA) using the UIPath platform. I have developed automation solutions to streamline repetitive and time-consuming tasks, thereby increasing efficiency and reducing manual errors. I have experience in creating UIPath workflows, configuring robots, and integrating automation processes with external systems.

As a UIPath developer, I have collaborated with business stakeholders to identify automation opportunities, analyze processes, and develop customized solutions. I am familiar with orchestrator setup and management, attended bots, and exception handling techniques. By leveraging my expertise in RPA, I have helped organizations optimize their workflows and achieve significant time and cost savings.

Overall, as a Full Stack Web Developer and UIPath RPA specialist, I bring a comprehensive skill set to the table. With a focus on delivering high-quality, scalable, and user-centric solutions, I am committed to driving innovation and efficiency in the development process.

Services

What I Do

  • 01

    Web Design

    I specialize in designing websites that are aesthetically pleasing, modern, and aligned with your brand identity. I create visually engaging layouts, select appropriate color schemes, and ensure intuitive navigation for an optimal user experience.

  • 02

    User Interface (UI)

    I focus on designing intuitive and user-friendly interfaces that enhance usability and engage website visitors. I carefully craft elements such as buttons, forms, menus, and typography to create an intuitive and visually appealing interface that aligns with your brand's style.

  • 03

    Responsive Design

    In today's mobile-driven world, it is crucial to have websites that adapt seamlessly to different screen sizes and devices. I ensure that all websites I design are responsive, providing a consistent and enjoyable user experience across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

  • 04

    Wireframing and Prototyping

    Before diving into the visual design, I create wireframes and interactive prototypes to establish the structure and functionality of the website. This helps in getting early feedback and ensuring that the final design meets your requirements.

  • 05

    Collaboration and Communication

    I work closely with clients to understand their goals, target audience, and specific design requirements. Throughout the design process, I maintain open communication to ensure that your vision is translated into an exceptional web design solution.

  • 06

    Website Optimization

    I pay attention to performance optimization, ensuring that the websites I design are fast-loading and optimized for search engines. I also consider accessibility standards to make sure that your website is inclusive and usable for all users.
